Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

BudgetApp is a client-side personal finance tool. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is stored and how it is used.

2. Data We Store

BudgetApp stores your budget entries (titles and amounts) exclusively in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

3. Cookies & Local Storage

We use localStorage for two purposes:

entry_list — stores your income and expense records so they persist between sessions.

cookie_accepted — records whether you have acknowledged this privacy notice, so the banner is not shown on every visit.

No tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party cookies are used.

4. Third-Party Services

BudgetApp does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services. All functionality runs locally in your browser.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is stored only on your own device. You can delete all stored data at any time by clearing your browser's localStorage (DevTools → Application → Local Storage → Clear).

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please raise an issue on our GitHub repository.
